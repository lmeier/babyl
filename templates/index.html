Why Babyl

With the internet, there ought to be better communication and more great writing now than ever before--the internet connects us with our friends, families, communities, and over 3 billion people. But instead, the internet often feels just like a place for vanity, headlines, and those making a quick buck.

Babyl is designed to encourage authentic writing and sharing. Ordinary social media has proven not to be the place for this. When likes and comments are on every post, sharing becomes inherently competetive. And when there's competetion, lowest common denominator content dominates.

With Babyl, you can share your writing worry-free. There is no attention sucking news-feed on Babyl. People only see your writing when they come to your Babyl, or, if you allow subscriptions, in their digest email.


What type of writing is Babyl good for?


  • The story (or stories!) you're writing
  • A travelogue
  • Your diary (this is our favorite!). Have deeply personal, written conversations with the people you're closest to
  • A learning log -- cement that knowledge!
  • Anything that you want to share

Why the name Babyl.online?

Babylon (get it?!) was one of the world's first great cities, and a home to rich culture, scholarship, and other writing, and so a fitting inspiration for our attempt to revive writing culture online.

The word 'babel' also traces its origins to Babylon. In an Old Testament story, the people of Babylon were made to speak different languages by God after disobeying him, resulting in complete confusion. Babyl.online is a home for confused writers--and we think just about everyone is confused in some way (and should be a writer!).

Babyl allows for written imperfection and confusion to be put in front a friendly audience. It's in those circumstances that we can best learn, grow, and live.
